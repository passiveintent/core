Important note on thresholds

Propensity thresholds are not universal constants.

A 3-page checkout funnel and a 40-page discovery catalog have entirely different structural probabilities.

The thresholds below assume a calibrated baseline tuned to your site's traffic patterns. For best accuracy, ensure your integration includes a calibration step before you apply hard intervention rules in production.
