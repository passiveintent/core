SaaS pricing & monetization

Don't guess their intent. Calculate it.

Standard analytics tell you a user abandoned the pricing page. PassiveIntent calculates a continuous, real-time Propensity to Buy score locally on the device. By combining bounded Markov chain probabilities with real-time kinematic variance, the engine predicts conversion drops before the user clicks away. Only trigger discounts for the users mathematically proven to be on the fence.
