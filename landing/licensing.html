Commercial use

You need a commercial license if PassiveIntent ships inside a proprietary product, runs in a network service without releasing the full app under AGPLv3, or is resold or white-labeled inside an analytics or AdTech product.

Commercial requests

If you need pilot access, procurement help, or a custom commercial arrangement, contact support@passiveintent.dev.

OEM / agency

Standard tiers are for internal business use. Agencies, SDK vendors, Shopify apps, white-label products, and other wrapped-product scenarios need a separate OEM or agency license.
