FAQ

What eCommerce teams ask before they install.

Does PassiveIntent require cookie banners to work?

For the default zero-egress, in-memory deployment, no.* If you enable persistent browser storage or export graph data to your own infrastructure, your consent and privacy obligations depend on your implementation and legal review.

Will Shadow Mode trigger popups on our storefront?

No. Shadow Mode is silent. It observes the same on-device math for 14 days and returns an audit before you decide whether to activate interventions.

Can this work on Shopify, BigCommerce, or a custom headless stack?

Yes. The integration path is an agnostic JavaScript snippet designed to work with standard storefront platforms and custom frontends.

Why is this different from Hotjar or Yieldify?

Hotjar-style tools tell you what happened after the loss. Yieldify-style tools still pay a server-latency penalty. PassiveIntent predicts hesitation locally in under 2 milliseconds and reacts while the session is still recoverable.
